What Preventative Maintenace Should We Be Concerned About?

It may sound counter intuitive, but some vehicle parts such as belts and hoses are meant to wear out over time. They are built to be soft and pliable so they can do their job properly. As such, they must be replaced every so often.

Preventative maintenance aims to fix key vehicle parts so we don't have to about fixing more expensive vehicle parts.

Here in Kern County, we are a desert community. Summers are hot, often going many days above 100degrees F, and winters, thout not snowy, are very cold.
